Date: November 2025

The Dumbbell Nebula

The Dumbbell Nebula (M27), located in the constellation Vulpecula, is a planetary nebula located about 1300 light years away. The remnants of an old star that shed its outer layers.

Equipment:

  • Seestar S50 Telescope
  • Total integration time: 3 minutes
