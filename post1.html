Date: November 2025

The Dumbbell Nebula

The Dumbbell Nebula (M27), located in the constellation Vulpecula, is a planetary nebula located about 1300 light years away. The remnants of an old star that shed its outer layers. This nebula is one of the easiest to spot and bightest planetary nebula in the night sky making it an easy target.

Equipment:

  • Seestar S50 Telescope
  • Exposure lengh: 10 seconds
  • Total integration time: 3 minutes
